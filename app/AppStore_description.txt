How many emails there is in your Gmail account inbox right now ?
"Inbox" let you clean your Gmail account by sorting your emails
in folders just as easily as you would put real mails to boxes.

Emails pops as you sort them, keep the emails you want to sort
later on your desk and just drag others where you want.
If the subject is not enough to decide, tap on an email to read it!  

With the offline mode, it's easy to sort your emails even where
you don't have a reliable connection !
